About

Some information about me.

  • 2006-2010

    What I started

    I created a site called MotorMindInc to create a small startup to fix computers. When that became difficult while working 2 jobs, I started making websites for people.

  • 2010

    Move and learn.

    The decision was made to dive deeper in to web development with College Courses. This also caused a rename to Web Design and Tech, since I could build or fix computers, fix or set up networks, and set up electronics in your house or office.

  • December 2011

    Associates Degree Obtained

    The risk was taken that I stay debt free with my Associates, since there was no guarantee in the Tri-City Area that $50,000 in debt for a Computer Science degree would get me a job.

  • April 2013

    First time hired as a Professional Developer

    I was hoping for a better offer, but I wanted experience no matter the cost. $13.42/hr and a 75-mile commute to Pendleton, Oregon for a new career.
