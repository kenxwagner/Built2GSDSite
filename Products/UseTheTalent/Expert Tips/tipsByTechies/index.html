Computers and Laptops

Preferred laptop brands: L.A.T.A - Lenovo, Asus, Toshiba, and Acer. Some other brands make high marks and are worth purchasing as well. CNET Laptops

Super tip... Backup your files and reset your laptop once in a while for best performance.

Tech tip: Run chkdsk (physical scanning of hard drive) with options to repair set to on. This takes a long time but prevents errors and screens of death for your computer. The hard drive has billions/trillions of spots on the a spinning disk where it stores data. One bad section can cause major issues, so the check disk is like a map you would use to avoid pot holes in the road to avoid issues like a flat tire.
