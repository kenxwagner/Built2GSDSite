Welcome to EdisonFinder.com!

My goal is to use one of the greatest tools available to create of network for inventors, entrepreneurs, tinkerers, and those who enjoy building, thinking, and creating everything that exists in this world. For athletes, actors, and musicians there was a way to get somewhere by networking or having a way to show what you can do. For athletes and entertainers, there are clear places to go and locations to prove your skill. Yet, for all the importance of creativity and ideas, there is no real outlet for people. After many years of trying, it finally dawned on me to just invent what didn't exist.

If you think there are ways to bring ideas to life, name them. Most of the advertisements for "Inventor's Wanted" belong to predatory scam companies intent on manipulation of a person's ignorance of the patent process. The other chunk of invention lookers belongs to patent troll companies.

There are two main schools of thought for would be "Edisons". The idea pushed most often is to take your best idea and to spend time and money on getting a patent, marketing it, prototyping, etc. And if you are lucky, you might see a chance of success from the thousands of dollars you spent on it. Stats thrown around carelessly are commonly claiming a 2-3% success rate for this method. I tried that for years and found it frustrating (especially with no money). Lo and behold, a few books and dozens's of websites later I find the book From Patent to Profit and things start clicking. Which leads to the other school of thought (the one this site is based on). This wild idea is to network with people who have connections (manufacturing, etc.) and experience in the area of the idea. To back it up, look at Eli Whitney, Thomas Edison, Alexander Graham Bell, George Washington Carver,  etc. None of them invented "One" great idea, they did far more. This second method also answers a problem I had for years with the other method. Namely, if I have lots of ideas, how do I know which one is the best one?

Problem solved! This website is for the Portland Metropolitan area. Network and invent to your hearts content. This is networking only for MEMBERS. To be a member you must be nominated and reviewed. Companies on the banned list (patent scam companies and patent trolls) are forbidden from this site, as are anyone openly claiming to represent them. The hope is that by creating a network for Inventors, Helpers, and Businesses; intellectual property will be given fertile soil in which to grow.

More to come!

January 26, 2008
