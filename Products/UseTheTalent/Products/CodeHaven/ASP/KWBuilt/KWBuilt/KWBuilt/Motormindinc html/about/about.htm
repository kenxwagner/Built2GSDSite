About MotormindInc.

Want a new job or career, but can't get your foot in the door, because you don't have enough "experience"? Have talent that you can't use because you haven't done the work with a title? It's is very frustrating and prevents talented people from doing what they like or doing what they are best at. This site is designed to help with that.

Purpose

To find work experience through volunteer work and independent contracting for those who seek. Many businesses will accept such work, but it is just the start. Tracking work hours and random testing will be added to help create a quality pool of talent. A secondary hope, is for those interested in pursuing a specific career to be able to try it out (by volunteering) without having to invest time and money only to find out it is the wrong fit.

Why? 

  • This is my solution to a problem that has hounded me for many years. I received many accolades for my performance and talents, but without the right circumstances and networking, I could not convert it into a better career. In some cases, I found myself leaving the job because outperforming my superiors.

  • I have the ability to change myself and it is something I will leave for others... if they so choose.

  • “Do not go where the path may lead; go instead where there is no path and leave a trail” - Ralph Waldo Emerson
