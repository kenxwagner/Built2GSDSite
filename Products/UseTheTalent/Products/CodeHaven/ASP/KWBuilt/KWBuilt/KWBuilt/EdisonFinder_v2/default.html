Welcome to Edison Finder

You have now entered a site which has the goal of increasing creativity and innovation by creating practical networking and activities for the benefit of inventors and the buyers/consumers of inventions.

How it should work.

This wild idea is to network with people who have connections (manufacturing, etc.) and experience in the area of the idea. To back it up, look at Eli Whitney, Thomas Edison, Alexander Graham Bell, George Washington Carver, etc. None of them invented "One" great idea, they did far more. This second method also answers a problem I had for years with the other method. Namely, if I have lots of ideas, how do I know which one is the best one? I created this site to help inventors do just that.
