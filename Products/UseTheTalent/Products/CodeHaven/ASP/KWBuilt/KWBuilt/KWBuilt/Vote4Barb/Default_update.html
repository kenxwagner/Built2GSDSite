Re-Elect Barbara Wagner for Benton County Assessor

Welcome!

Thank you for visiting my website. It is a great honor to serve as your Benton County Assessor, and to lead an outstanding team of dedicated public employees.

I want to thank you for all the past support. I look forward to the next four year’s serving you as your Benton County Assessor.

Sincerely,

Barbara Wagner

Reasons to Vote for Barbara Wagner:

  • Experience - Long-time experience, current Assessor since 1991 responsible for a 1.9 million dollar annual budget.
  • Management Skills – Currently responsible for a staff of 25 employees; already trained with numerous management training courses.
  • Appraiser - Accredited appraiser since 1976, with a current inactive Real Estate License since 1975.
  • Full -Time - Barbara has always been your full-time Assessor and will take no other part time job, as this position pays for a full time elected official.
  • Property Owner - Barbara can relate to property owners, since she is a current Benton County property owner today and going back to 1976.
  • Results – Proven results of uniformity and equity of property taxes in Benton County. Do a search for Measuring Real Property Appraisal performance in Washington’s property tax system; see the perfect score for last 5 years in a row for the Benton County Assessor’s office.
  • Leadership – Proven leadership Yesterday, Today and Tomorrow. Barbara has been entrusted with numerous leadership rolls. She is currently the President of Washington Association of County Officials; and has previously been past President of the Washington State Association of County Assessor’s.
