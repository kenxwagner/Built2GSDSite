How it works

You have skills that you would like to develop or get experience for those skills, then you need a place to work on them. If you live in the area of Washington State in or near Kennewick, Richland, or Pasco, Select an option below.

Skills Request:

I am an organization or business looking for talented/skilled individuals who can volunteer or file a 1099 contract form.

Skills Supply:

I am an individual or business looking to show or use my talents/skills either through volunteer or 1099 contract.

Others:

I am an looking to join, but live too far away. Sorry, I am not expanding yet. Areas available will be announced when they are ready. Send an e-mail with your contact information to MotorMindInc@gmail.com and I will try to update you.

I am in need of your help/services to fix something. Sorry, we do not deal with any charity work, etc except through reputable organizations and a contact point through such groups.
