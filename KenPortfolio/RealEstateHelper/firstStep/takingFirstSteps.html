House buying rules.

Here are the essential steps for getting your own home.

First Step

Find a lender and get pre-approved, unless you already have the cash.

You have to know how much you can buy or you are wasting your time and the realtor's time.

Helpful tip: do not go for the maximum unless you want to be house poor.
