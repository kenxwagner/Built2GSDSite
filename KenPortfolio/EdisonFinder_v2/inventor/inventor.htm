Inventors

This is a members only website for those interested in pursuing and creating Intellectual Property. This is limited to the Tri-City Metro Area and sometimes more of the Northwest itself.

To be a member, you must be affiliated with a member that will vouch for you. To be in Inventor category, you must be actively pursuing ideas. If you wish to pass the "idea" on to someone else and just want to sell it, you won't get far.

Barring legal precedence, it should be okay for you to be in any of the categories you want.

The goal here is to post all your ideas in a vague format such as this:

Name: Thomas Edison    
   
  Vague Title Prototype Field
1 Telegraph advancement Yes Telegraphy
       
2 Telephone improvement Yes Communications
       
3 Artificial Light Yes Electrical
       
4 Ore separation No Mining
      Metallurgy
       
5 Music device   Music
