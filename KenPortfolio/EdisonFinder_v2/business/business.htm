Businesses

Welcome to the business page.

To be a member, you must be affiliated with a member that will vouch for you. Those who have monetary involvement are in this category. If one decides to help based on contingency (in other words, free help, prototyping, etc. at first, but a payoff later IF the ideas comes to fruition), then that would make you a helper as well (at least in regards to a specific invention). If you are dealing with money up front, then you will want to be dealing as a business. However, barring legal precedence, it should be okay for you to be in any of the categories you want.

*****NOTE: This website is for networking. Legal protection via agreements and contracts are the sole responsibility of the parties involved.*****
