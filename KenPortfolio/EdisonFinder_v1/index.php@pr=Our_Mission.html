EdisonFinder.com's Mission

As the name implies, the goal is to find all the Thomas Edison's out in the world. However, as Confucious said: "A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step." I am starting this site here in the Portland, Oregon area.

The thought is like this: Problem --> Idea --> Research and Development --> Patents --> Prototypes --> Production --> Marketing --> Sales --> Distribution --> End User.

That is a lot for one person. If you spent two years on that, you'd spend loads of cash, time and effort.

To copy off of Bob DeMattis: Inventor --> Market Contact --> Business that wants idea.
Also: Inventor --> Helper --> Prototype ---> Market Contact --> Business that wants idea.
Or: Business --> Edisonfinder.com --> Inventor.

With the greatest networking tool ever created at our disposal, it occurred to me to just "invent" a website using techniques far less difficult than the go it alone inventor would use.

To aid in the ease of use, this is a members only sight and for those located in the Portland Metro area.
