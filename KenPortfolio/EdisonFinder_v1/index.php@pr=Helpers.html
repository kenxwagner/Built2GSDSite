Welcome Helpers

This is a members only website for those interested in pursuing and creating Intellectual Property. This is limited to the Portland Metro Area and sometimes more of the Northwest itself.

To be a member, you must be affiliated with a member that will vouch for you. To be a Helper the criteria is broad, offering advice, expertise, information, prototype help, research, ideas you don't wish to pursue yourself, legal advice, etc. Here's the kicker: This category is for those that do not immediately charge for their work/help. If one decides to help based on contingency (in other words, free help, prototyping, etc. at first, but a payoff later IF the ideas comes to fruition), then that would make you a helper as well. If you are dealing with money up front, then you will want to be dealing as a business. However, barring legal precedence, it should be okay for you to be in any of the categories you want.

*****NOTE: This website is for networking. Legal protection via agreements and contracts are the sole responsibility of the parties involved.*****
