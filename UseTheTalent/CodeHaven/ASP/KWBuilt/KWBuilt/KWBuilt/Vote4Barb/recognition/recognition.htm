Washington Association of County Officials (WACO) is a nonprofit organization that provides legislative representation to its members and coordinating agency for seven county official groups. Membership includes county assessors, auditors, county clerks, coroners, prosecuting attorneys, sheriffs and county treasurers in each of Washington’s 39 counties.

2004-2006 WSACA Chairperson Education Committee
2000 WSACA President
1999 WSACA Vice-President
1998 WSACA Secretary-Treasurer

Washington State Association of County Assessors (WSACA) is an organization of all 39 elected County Assessors in the State of Washington. This organization has been in existence for over 100 years.

Professional County Official

Barbara received her document for Certified Professional County Official in 1999 from the University of Washington. This award was obtained after numerous accumulations of both management classes and courses relating in particular to the “Elected Office of Assessor“ as well as tenure in the elected position.
