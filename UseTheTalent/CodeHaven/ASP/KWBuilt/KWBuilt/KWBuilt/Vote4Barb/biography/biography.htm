Re-Elect Barbara Wagner for Benton County Assessor

Biography

Barbara Wagner grew up in Portland Oregon. She came to the Tri-Cities when her husband accepted a job offer, which brought them here in 1975.

After graduating from Marshall High School and attending Portland State University for two years as a PE Major, she changed her path at a young age and started taking classes in Real Estate Law at Columbia Basin College.

Once she obtained her Real Estate License on 04/09/1975, she worked under Broker Ernie Boston in Richland. Barbara still maintains an inactive status Real Estate License to this day.

In 1976 Barbara was hired by the Benton County Assessor’s office, and was trained and passed the necessary test to become an appraiser for Benton County. For 15 years Barbara was an appraiser in the Benton County Assessor’s Office, when her boss decided to retire. With 15 years of experience as a government appraiser and familiarity with all aspects of the office and knowing the entire staff she ran for the position of Assessor and won a 1990 election that put her in the Elected Assessor position January 1991.

Barbara is a widow; she has two adult children and three grandchildren. She has always been a community involved volunteer.

In the past Barbara has contributed to the following activities:

1995 Kennewick High parent music booster President
1994 Kennewick High parent music booster Treasurer
1992 - 1995 Kennewick High parent music booster Chaperon
1990 Boys Park and Rec Softball Coach
1988 Northwest Pee-Wee Rodeo Association President
1984 Girls Park and Rec Basketball Coach
1983 Boys and Girls Little League in Finley Coach
